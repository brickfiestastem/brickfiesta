About

Brick Fiesta is the premier fan of LEGO event held in the great state of Texas. Since 2003 the idea of having a LEGO convention and exhibition has been the dream and ambition of several TexLUG members. With the opening of a LEGO store in Austin in 2011 and the right people asking the right questions Brick Fiesta is coming soon near you. We are looking forward to years of continued success.
